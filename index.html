About Greenspiration

Here at Greenspiration we believe that small steps, have a big impact and that together we can enact real change. But reducing your carbon footprint can feel overwhelming, time-consuming, expensive and like a drop in the ocean compared with the enormity of the climate crisis.

Constantly tracking and analysing your carbon footprint is not sustainable. Using Greenspiration is! Each week we cover just one key source of carbon emissions, summarise the latest research into a concise and accessible format so everyone can become climate literate. .

Your feedback is important to us. By sharing a challenge or barrier you have faced when trying to reduce your carbon footprint, we gain a clearer understanding of climate privilege and where support is needed.
