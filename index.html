Welcome to your Weekly Task!

This week's theme is transport. How much carbon emissions do you transmit when you're travelling? Lets find out 😃

Did you know a medium petrol car transmits 192g of carbon per km? Car sharing is a great scheme to help reduce emissions while travelling to work. Just car sharing with one extra passenger would half your carbon footprint to 96g per km. Even better, travelling by train reduces your carbon footprint even more! Travelling by train only transmits per passenger 41g of carbon per km. Where possible, the best method of transport is walking or cycling, as this has no effect on your carbon footprint, but this can sometimes prove difficult due to distance, disability, and many other reasons. Where possible, the train is the best mode of transport to get to work, if walking and cycling are not an option.
